Chapter 113 - Uncle Wang, Send Someone to Mop Up
==================================================

"Holy crap, this stuff actually works?! What a treasure!"

Chen Shu had always thought the potion was useless junk. Who knew the system reward would be this overpowered?

Two minutes later, the Slime's body reappeared.

"I only used a tenth of it just now. That means the potion gives twenty minutes of invisibility!"

Chen Shu mulled it over, then took a sip himself. Sure enough, his body vanished too.

He recalled the Slime and sprinted toward the dark alley.

Time was limited!

He quickly reached the end of the alley. To his surprise, it led to a three-story building with a courtyard.

Creak!

Chen Shu gently pushed open the gate, about to step inside.

"Hm?"

A voice suddenly rang out.

Chen Shu froze, rooted to the spot, terrified of being discovered.

"Why didn't you guys lock the door?"

Footsteps approached.

Before the man got close, Chen Shu slipped into the courtyard. He immediately spotted eight people gathered there.

Baldy Liu had taken off his pink winter coat and was complaining. "This thing's hot as hell to wear. Makes me look like a lunatic."

A middle-aged man spoke up. "It was all to lure the Spirit Suppression Bureau here."

"Brother Li, are we really going to kill Spirit Suppression agents?"

A young man's eyes betrayed his fear and unease.

The bespectacled man said, "Only by stirring up chaos in Nanjiang can we slip away unnoticed."

Right now, the Spirit Suppression Bureau thought Baldy Liu was operating alone. Even if they came to arrest him, they wouldn't send many people.

If they got counter-ambushed, the Bureau would suffer devastating casualties!

"So what now? That student who escaped saw all of us. The Bureau won't give us another chance."

"There's still time. Let's finalize our escape plan!"

Chen Shu listened from the side, surprised by how vicious these people were.

He scanned his surroundings but didn't see Xie Sunan or Xu Xingxing.

While the invisibility potion was still active, he slipped into the three-story building. In the innermost room on the first floor, he found the two of them unconscious.

"That makes things easier."

Chen Shu summoned the Golden Slime and fed it the entire remaining potion.

"Man, they really forced my hand here!"

The invisible Slime glided silently into the courtyard.

"Roll out, Slime!"

Chen Shu didn't use the Gigantify skill. A Slime over ten meters tall could easily demolish the building and might hurt the surrounding residents.

Besides, he wanted to toy with these bastards a little.

The invisible Slime immediately activated its Charge skill, barreling toward the group.

The bespectacled man was in the middle of an impassioned speech, his face brimming with positive energy.

"Everyone, don't be pessimistic! We need to stay proactive, really commit to collectivization, scaling up, building a franchise. In three years, we break even. In five years, we double our profits. In ten years, we secure funding and go public! Together, we'll create a brand new fu—"

WHAM!

Mid-speech, the man's body twisted grotesquely, as if he'd been hit by a semi-truck.

He went flying and slammed into the wall, his body crumpling to the ground like a ragdoll, twitching sporadically.

"???"

Everyone stood there stupefied. What the hell was this?

Someone called out, "Brother Li, quit messing around! Get up, we need to talk about the funding!"

"What acting?! His intestines are hanging out!"

Someone finally caught on, scanning the area with alarm.

WHAM!

Another thunderous impact. Another body went airborne, coughing up blood mid-flight, face contorted in agony.

"Enemy attack!"

Finally, everyone snapped to attention and summoned their contract spirits.

This group were all at least Grade 8 Beast Tamers. Not only were they powerful individually, their coordination was seamless—otherwise they never could've survived this long in Nanjiang City.

One of them ducked behind his contract spirit and breathed a quiet sigh of relief.

But at that moment, he felt a rush of wind at his back—then he was sent flying too.

"It's an invisible contract spirit! Attack everywhere!"

Baldy Liu reacted fastest. He curled up in the corner of the courtyard, his Black-Tailed Scorpion launching attacks at everything in front of him.

Invisibility didn't mean invincibility. It just meant you couldn't be seen—damage could still get through.

Chen Shu watched from inside the building, a smile tugging at his lips.

The invisible Slime suddenly stopped and activated its new exclusive skill: Sleep Kill!

Zzzzz... Zzzzz...

Snoring filled the courtyard. The surviving criminals were utterly baffled.

"What the hell is that?"

Confusion flickered in Baldy Liu's eyes. He had no idea what was happening.

But he could hear the snoring getting louder, as if something was approaching him.

WHAM!

The Black-Tailed Scorpion's tail made contact with the Slime—but couldn't pierce its defense.

"It's here?!"

Baldy Liu instantly commanded the Scorpion to use its skill. A dark gleam appeared on its tail—but it was already too late.

WHAM!

The Slime kept snoring while it rolled forward. Baldy Liu died on impact.

No ordinary human could withstand a flesh-bullet collision.

Now the remaining criminals were truly terrified. The snoring sounded like Death itself calling.

With its defense through the roof, the Slime tanked attacks from the other contract spirits while bouncing around the courtyard.

In less than five minutes, the entire criminal gang was dead!

All of the Slime's stats surged by 20%. Its power had grown significantly once again.

"Yo! Uncle Wang! Send someone to mop up!"

Chen Shu called Wang Qian and gave him the location.

He recalled the Slime, propped up Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing—one under each arm—and hauled them back to school.

...

The next day.

"Please, sir! Don't kill me! I've got an eighty-year-old mother and a three-year-old still breastfeeding..."

Xie Sunan jolted awake and shot up from his bed.

"Dude, was that really necessary?"

Chen Shu had been woken up and gave Xie Sunan an exasperated look.

Xie Sunan blinked in confusion. "Huh? I'm back in the dorm? Was it all a dream?"

He remembered falling off the Slime and getting captured by the criminals.

"Of course it wasn't a dream!"

Chen Shu yawned. "With me around, nothing was going to happen."

After several rounds of explanation, the two finally—reluctantly—believed that Chen Shu had rescued them.

Chen Shu picked up his phone. There were already over a dozen missed calls.

"Hey! Uncle Wang! I was exhausted last night. Passed out the second my head hit the pillow."

"You slept like a baby while I was up all night! Tell me the truth—how did you take out that whole gang?"

"They killed each other. Someone asked whether the chicken or the egg came first, they couldn't agree, the argument got heated, and they all ended up dead."

"......" Wang Qian fell into a long silence.

"Uncle Wang, don't I at least deserve a Model Citizen award for this?"

Wang Qian replied, "You'll be rewarded properly. Right now the Bureau's going crazy because of these criminals—turns out every single one of them was on the wanted list!"

"Well, don't let me keep you!"

Chen Shu hung up and headed to class with Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing.

After last night's ordeal, the three of them had grown closer.

Joke all you want, mess around if you dare—when push comes to shove, the Nanjiang Bandit's there!

...

"Today we're officially distributing the rewards from yesterday's competition!"

Shen Wushuang stood at the front of the classroom. "When I call your name, come up to claim your prize!"

"First place: Chen Shu!"

At this, everyone wore various expressions. Getting beaten by an F-rank Slime for the championship really did mess with your head.

Chen Shu smiled and nodded, surveying every face in the class.

"First place: Chen Shu!"

Yet still, no one went up to the stage.

Shen Wushuang's mouth twitched as he looked down at the students. "Chen Shu, are you deaf?"

Chen Shu replied with a straight face, "I could hear. I just wanted to make sure everyone ELSE heard."

"......"
