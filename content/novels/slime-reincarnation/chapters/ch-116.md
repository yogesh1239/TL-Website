Chapter 116: This Is a Notice, Not a Negotiation
=====================================

Who could possibly say no to being a class monitor with a 200,000+ yuan monthly salary?

Chen Shu rubbed his hands together and shot Shen Wushuang a look full of implications.

Shen Wushuang's face went blank with exasperation. He pulled out two High-Grade Beast Orbs on the spot.

"Rest assured, classmates! I will carry out my duties with utmost diligence!"

Suddenly, Wang Yun spoke up, "Teacher, aren't you playing favorites here?"

Shen Wushuang replied flatly, "Everything is based on ability. If you can beat him, the class monitor position is yours."

He turned to Chen Shu. "Start teaching."

Chen Shu looked confused. "Teaching? What teaching?"

"???"

Shen Wushuang's eye twitched. *This guy's trying to weasel out of it right in front of me?*

"Teacher, I think that's a separate fee."

"Oh, forget it. Give me the orbs back!"

Shen Wushuang walked over to him, ready to snatch them back by force.

"I'll teach! I'll teach!"

Chen Shu immediately capitulated. Once something landed in his hands, there was no way he'd let it be taken back.

"Xiaoyu, your Fire Bird's Fireball doesn't have to be launched all at once. You can release them based on the situation."

Chen Shu walked over to the Fire Bird and explained, "There are eight fireballs total. You can try holding them in your beak and see if you can combine them into one bigger fireball."

"Also, you don't have to rigidly spit them from your mouth. You could try other positions—like the butt..."

With that, Chen Shu fiddled with the Fire Bird's feathers, trying to see if fireballs could come out of its rear end.

SCREECH!

The Fire Bird flapped its wings and shot into the air, eyes full of outrage, screeching incessantly at Chen Shu.

*You're actually insane!*

Xu Xiaoyu was equally speechless. *What kind of Fireball technique comes out of the butt?*

"Xiaoyu, it's worth trying."

Shen Wushuang, however, brightened with interest and said this with complete seriousness.

"Teacher..." Xu Xiaoyu hadn't expected Shen Wushuang to be just as abnormal as Chen Shu.

"If it actually works, that would truly be unexpected. As long as you can achieve victory, it doesn't matter where the fireballs come from."

Shen Wushuang hadn't expected Chen Shu's ideas to be so... unconventional!

Chen Shu then moved to Tang Lie's side and began scrutinizing his Wind Runner Beast.

Though Tang Lie didn't want Chen Shu's guidance, it was the teacher's order, so he couldn't refuse.

Chen Shu considered for a moment, then said flatly, "No hope. Just reroll."

"..."

*My A-rank contract spirit, and you're telling me to reroll?!*

Chen Shu proceeded to comment on everyone else's contract spirit skills as well.

They didn't necessarily have to practice exactly as he said—the point was to use this as a springboard for them to learn to think for themselves.

Of course, thinking was one thing, but actually doing it was another matter entirely.

Every student in the class had at least a B-rank contract spirit. While their potential was high, their obedience levels were generally low.

Only Chen Shu's slime could perfectly replicate his ideas. No way around it—the slime's intelligence... er... obedience was just too high.

The entire day, the class practiced controlling their contract spirits. This was something every Apprentice Beast Tamer had to master.

...

"Let's go, time for food!"

Chen Shu and his two companions left the Beast Taming Arena.

"Hold on, I need to pick something up at the school gate."

Chen Shu and Xie Sunan stared blankly. A black sports car had pulled up at the school entrance, drawing frequent glances from passing students.

An elderly man in a black tailcoat stepped out, holding a small box in his hands.

"Young Master! Here are the Beast Taming Pearls you requested!"

Xu Xingxing took the box from the old man's hands and immediately ran back into campus.

"Holy crap, domineering CEO much?"

Chen Shu's eyes went wide as he stared at Xu Xingxing in shock.

Xie Sunan asked, "Xiao Xing, do you have stomach problems? Chronic insomnia? A cleanliness obsession? Is your real surname secretly 'Gu'?"[1]

"Huh? The hell are you talking about?"

Xu Xingxing was stunned, having no idea what Xie Sunan was getting at.

Chen Shu followed up with a curious question: "Xiao Xing, when you're constipated, do you say with a cold expression: 'Hmph, come out on your own!'?"

"You two are seriously deranged!" Xu Xingxing had never been this speechless in his life.

Outside the school gate, the elderly man watched Xu Xingxing and murmured, "It's been so long since I've seen the Young Master laugh like this."

...

"So Xiao Xing, with your family being this loaded, why are you still doing part-time work at a psychiatric hospital?"

Chen Shu accepted the Beast Taming Pearls Xu Xingxing handed him—seven in total, worth approximately 1.05 million yuan, roughly the same as the prize Shen Wushuang had given out.

"Chen Pi, what are you even saying? We're the ones doing part-time work. Xiao Xing is just experiencing life."

The three joked and messed around, not treating Xu Xingxing any differently because of his family background.

Chen Shu had his system. Even if he did nothing and just lay flat every day, he'd still become powerful.

And Xie Sunan had his divine skill in hand. No matter how rich someone was, they'd still have to fear him.

Chen Shu returned to the dorm and swallowed all seven Beast Taming Pearls. They had no particular taste and dissolved instantly upon entering his mouth.

His Beast Taming Power surged dramatically, bringing him one step closer to Level 8 Beast Tamer.

The higher a Beast Tamer's level, the harder it was to break through.

Xu Xiaoyu came from a wealthy family, yet she'd been stuck at Level 7 for several months.

Xu Xingxing was also only a Level 7 Beast Tamer. Unless his family spent tens of millions to buy him Beast Taming Pearls.

But with that kind of money, why not buy Lord-tier fierce beasts to comprehend skills instead?

While Chen Shu was resting in the dorm, a call suddenly came through.

"Hello, Uncle Wang?"

"Come to the Spirit Suppression Bureau tomorrow. There's something I need to discuss with you!"

*Could it be bonus time?*

Chen Shu put away his phone, sent Shen Wushuang a message, and immediately fell asleep.

Meanwhile, Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing sat in meditation, cultivating to increase their Beast Taming Power.

Early the next morning, the special training class gathered at the Beast Taming Arena again.

"In September, your task is to master flexible use of skills. Next month, I'll take you to train in an Alternate Dimension! Only by learning to use your skills properly can you ensure your own safety!"

At these words, everyone's expressions grew excited, eyes full of anticipation.

Most of the class had never been to an Alternate Dimension before—they'd only heard about it.

Shen Wushuang continued, "During this period, I hope everyone will train diligently! Next month's competition results will be based on your performance in the Alternate Dimension!"

"Class monitor, supervise everyone!"

Shen Wushuang called out, but he couldn't spot Chen Shu anywhere.

"Hm? Where's Chen Shu?"

Xie Sunan reported, "Mr. Shen, Chen Shu requested leave."

Shen Wushuang frowned. "Leave? Why wasn't I told?"

"He said he sent you a ChaoXin message yesterday."

Shen Wushuang pulled out his phone. Sure enough, Chen Shu had left him a message.

"Mr. Shen, I will probably be sick tomorrow, hereby requesting one day of leave. Note: This is a notice, not a negotiation!"[2]

---

[1] **Domineering CEO (霸道总裁)**: A massively popular Chinese romance novel genre featuring cold, wealthy male leads with formulaic traits: stomach problems, insomnia, cleanliness obsessions, and disproportionately the surname "Gu" (顾). The constipation joke parodies how these characters speak in commanding tones even in absurd situations.

[2] **"This is a notice, not a negotiation"**: A catchphrase from the 2022 comedy sketch "少爷和我" (The Young Master and I) from the variety show "一年一度喜剧大赛" (Annual Comedy Competition). The sketch satirized domineering CEO tropes—connecting to the CEO parody earlier in this chapter.
