Chapter 117: Messenger of Love and Peace
=====================================

"Don't you dare face me in real life—if you've got the balls, come argue with me online!"

Chen Shu whistled as he strolled leisurely out of school.

Online, he wasn't even scared of Fang Si. What chance did a mere Shen Wushuang have?

"Driver! Spirit Suppression Bureau!"

Chen Shu hailed a taxi. His gaze swept over the driver and his expression shifted in shock. "Holy crap, driver—why the hell are you wearing chainmail?"

The driver rattled the chainmail on his body and laughed. "Safety. For safety!"

Chen Shu's mouth twitched. Your safety awareness is a bit extreme, buddy.

An hour later, Chen Shu arrived at the Spirit Suppression Bureau entrance.

Looking at the familiar scene, he couldn't help but recall being mistaken for a fake medicine peddler.

The moment he stepped inside—

Wang Qian asked with a serious expression, "Chen Shu, does your family own a tank?"

"??" Chen Shu's face went blank. "What?"

"All eight criminals' bodies were crushed by a heavy object. Preliminary forensics concluded someone drove a tank."

"Uncle Wang, give me a break!" Chen Shu's mouth twitched. "You think you can just drive a tank through the city?"

He hadn't expected Wang Qian's imagination to be so wild. With creativity like that, he should be writing novels and making a name for himself, not being a squad leader at the Spirit Suppression Bureau.

Wang Qian spoke up. "Alright, that's not why I called you here."

"We've identified who tried to have you killed!"

Chen Shu immediately asked, "Oh? Who?"

"Summer Star Potion Company, here in the city!"

Wang Qian said thoughtfully, "They're a major pharmaceutical company in Nanjiang City. Do you have some kind of business conflict with them?"

Chen Shu wore an expression of sudden understanding. As expected—he'd been targeted by a potion company.

"I just set up a little ground stall in the black market. Might've affected their business."

"That's impossible. How could one ground stall affect them?"

"Uh... my ground stall might be a bit bigger than average."

"Oh please. How big could a ground stall possibly be?"

Chen Shu thought back. "About two thousand-plus bottles of Strength Potion in ten days."

"Cough... cough..."

Wang Qian had been drinking water. He immediately choked.

"Is your ground stall a franchise?!"

Now he understood why Summer Star Potion Company had developed murderous intentions.

They had just changed their strategy, paid an enormous price, and were trying to monopolize Nanjiang City's Strength Potion market.

Then some student came along and collapsed the whole thing. Anyone would find that unacceptable.

Wang Qian said, "We need to gather evidence before we can arrest Summer Star's executives. Keep a low profile for now."

Since they'd broken the law, the Spirit Suppression Bureau could take them down no matter how big the company was.

"Also, all eight were wanted criminals. The bounty totals five hundred thousand Huaxia currency!"

With that, Wang Qian had someone transfer five hundred thousand to Chen Shu's bank account.

A greedy glint appeared in Chen Shu's eyes. "That's it? Weren't there eight of them?"

Wang Qian's mouth twitched. "Not every criminal has a hundred thousand bounty. Be grateful you got five hundred thousand."

Although they were all wanted criminals, only the Level 9 Beast Tamer had a hundred thousand bounty.

"Uncle Wang, I have a question." Chen Shu suddenly leaned in close and whispered, "Does the Bureau sell tanks? With shells included?"

Wang Qian's mouth twitched. "Get lost!"

Chen Shu took his newly acquired five hundred thousand and happily left the Spirit Suppression Bureau.

He looked up Summer Star Potion Company online and discovered their main product was Strength Potion.

Now that they were on the Spirit Suppression Bureau's radar, Summer Star wouldn't survive much longer.

When that happened, Nanjiang City's Strength Potion market would be wide open. As the Black Market's Big Bro of Strength Potions, he was definitely getting a piece of that pie!

Chen Shu dialed a number.

"Yo! Tianbao, my man."

"I don't need blood packets... Gather your boys and meet up. We're about to make some big moves!"

Half an hour later, Chen Shu met up with Wang Tianbao and his crew at a restaurant.

Besides Wang Tianbao, there were five others—all the guys who had helped Chen Shu run his ground stall.

"Tianbao, what've you been up to lately?"

Wang Tianbao thought for a moment. "Rectangular Prism Relocation Engineer."

"???" Chen Shu's expression blanked. "What kind of engineer?"

Wang Tianbao scratched his head. "Uh... moving bricks."

Chen Shu's mouth twitched. "Give me a break. What about your bros?"

"Little Wang is a Regional Security Inspector, Little Liu is a Shared Computing Chief Financial Officer, Little Zhang researches Human Epidermal Contaminants, and Little Deng is a Wilderness Survival Pioneer Scout..."

"Hold up, hold up."

Chen Shu rubbed his temples—his head was starting to hurt. "Speak human!"

Wang Tianbao gave an awkward laugh. "Uh... security guard, internet cafe worker, bath scrubber, and unemployed..."[1]

Chen Shu sighed. "Bro, can we keep it real? Enough with all this pretentious nonsense."

"Young Master Chen, what do you do?"

"Me? If we're being classy about it, I'm Nanjiang City's Messenger of Love and Peace."

"Huh? What?"

Everyone looked over, utterly confused.

Chen Shu said flatly, "The Nanjiang Bandit."

In an instant, everyone shot to their feet. Give me a break—so he's a criminal?!

Chen Shu quickly grabbed Wang Tianbao. "Wait! Wait! I'm a law-abiding citizen! I specialize in punishing evildoers—an evildoer who punishes evil!"

"So... 'evil squared' for short?"

Chen Shu's mouth twitched. "Alright, enough with the tangents. I've got real business for you."

"I'm planning to expand from my ground stall and rent an actual storefront in the black market—a shop specifically for Strength Potions. I want to hire you all as employees!"

At these words, everyone's interest was piqued.

The ground stall was only temporary work, never a long-term thing. But if it was a proper shop, they'd have steady jobs.

As long as there was money, even having the Nanjiang Bandit as their boss was fine.

Chen Shu then briefly outlined his shop plans. Since it was a potion shop, they'd obviously need a Potion Master and staff.

Chen Shu would naturally serve as the Potion Master himself. Hiring another one would be expensive, and other Potion Masters couldn't match his output anyway.

Since he had school to attend and limited time, the shop would only sell High-Grade Strength Potions.

For raw materials, Chen Shu could only source them from the Beast Tamer Association for now.

After everyone discussed the various details, they headed to the black market that afternoon.

"Is this shop for rent?"

After some consideration, Chen Shu decided to rent the shop in front of him. The size and location were both quite good.

A clerk stood up. "One moment, I need to get the owner."

"Oh? It's you?"

Chen Shu recognized the clerk instantly. This was the same shop where he'd first sold his Strength Potion—where his five-thousand-yuan potion had only fetched three thousand.

Most annoying of all, the guy had placed his potion under the five-thousand-yuan label right in front of him. Absolutely zero respect.

Chen Shu raised an eyebrow. "Is your shop going under?"

"Sigh. Summer Star Company has been running crazy promotions lately. The shop can't sell any Strength Potions anymore."

Chen Shu said with a grin, "Well, that's just so sad to hear."

[1] **Inflated Job Titles**: A Chinese internet meme trend where mundane jobs are described with absurdly corporate jargon. "Rectangular Prism Relocation Engineer" = brick layer, "Human Epidermal Contaminant Researcher" = bath scrubber. The format satirizes corporate culture's tendency to make simple jobs sound impressive.
