Chapter 114: I Can't Exactly Tell Them I've Got Cheats, Can I?

=====================================

"Chen Pi, get your ass up here!"

Shen Wushuang finally lost his composure. He'd never seen anyone this insufferable.

Only then did Chen Shu leisurely rise, nodding at his classmates with a smile. "That's right, that's right—I'm Chen Shu, the 'first place' Chen Shu!"

To emphasize his ranking, he deliberately put extra weight on those three words: "first place."

Black lines appeared on everyone's foreheads. This guy was asking for a beating.

But there was nothing they could do about it—they couldn't beat him in hand-to-hand combat, and their contract spirits were useless against him too. They could only accept their fate.

"Everyone's here, right? Let me say a few words!"

Chen Shu clasped his hands behind his back, adopting the posture of a leader, completely ignoring Shen Wushuang.

"Winning first place—I have to thank everyone here. If it weren't for all of you being so weak, how could I have won so easily?"

"..." The audience was speechless for a long moment.

"Especially you, Young Master Tang! I want to thank you for making me look so good by comparison!"

In his seat, Tang Lie clenched both fists, his face turning slightly green with rage.

In all his years, he'd never been insulted like this.

"Finally, I'd like to use one phrase to describe myself." Chen Shu cleared his throat and spoke with a completely serious expression.

"A sparrow pecking a cow's butt—truly fucking awesome!"[1]

"Get the hell off the stage!"

Shen Wushuang threw the box containing his reward directly at him.

If he let Chen Shu keep talking, the entire class would probably lose their minds.

"Wait, teacher, I haven't finished speaking—"

"Second place, Tang Lie!"

Shen Wushuang cut him off directly and called Tang Lie to the stage.

Chen Shu shook his head helplessly and returned to his seat.

"Oh, nice reward!"

Chen Shu opened the box in his hands. Inside were four white pearls and what appeared to be a fierce beast's internal organ.

He immediately recognized the four pearls as high-grade Beast Orbs.

Their black market value was around 120,000 yuan—about six times the price of low-grade Beast Orbs.

Four of them meant 480,000 yuan. Adding the mutant-level fierce beast organ beside them, the total value exceeded a million.

Of course, the school had official channels that would be cheaper, but he could take these to the black market and quickly convert them to Huaxia currency.

Whether it was fierce beast materials or Beast Orbs, they were hot commodities on the black market.

The fierce beast organ came from a mutant Roaring Lion from the Frost Ravine. Its innate skill was War Cry, which could briefly stun enemies—a pretty versatile skill overall.

"Holy crap, that's good stuff!"

Xie Sunan leaned over to look, rubbing his hands together, his face full of envy.

Chen Shu hadn't expected a class competition's first-place prize to be worth a million Huaxia yuan.

The school had really gone all out.

Of course, only the first-place reward was this generous. The second-place reward dropped significantly—only around 500,000 yuan.

Just as Shen Wushuang was distributing the rewards, a pot-bellied man appeared outside the classroom.

It was the principal of Nanjiang Second High!

"Teacher Shen, could you step outside for a moment?"

Shen Wushuang temporarily left the classroom.

"Teacher Shen, I know you have extensive teaching experience, but isn't giving such a large reward to an F-rank beast taming student a bit much?"

The principal frowned, a hint of displeasure in his eyes.

Although the school could allocate money to cultivate the special training class, good steel should be put where it counts. Pouring substantial resources into seeds destined for Huaxia Academy was acceptable.

However, Chen Shu's F-rank contract spirit could only get him into a prestigious university at best. Spending too many resources on him was clearly wasteful.

Shen Wushuang smiled and said, "Principal, Chen Shu may not be unable to get into Huaxia Academy. At least for now, he's shown himself to be better than everyone else."

The principal countered, "Do you think Huaxia Academy would accept someone with an F-rank contract spirit?"

Shen Wushuang's voice caught, and he fell silent.

The quality of one's first contract spirit was indeed crucial—it affected not only the beast tamer's combat power but also their lifetime achievements.

While there was no definitive conclusion, if someone's first contract spirit was S-rank, their second and third contract spirits generally wouldn't be far behind.

And since Chen Shu's first contract spirit was F-rank, that meant his second contract spirit would likely be E-rank or D-rank.

Would Huaxia Academy really accept such a student?

This was also why potential rating was such an important assessment criterion in the college entrance exam.

Shen Wushuang shook his head firmly. "Regardless, I have to maintain fairness. We can't have a situation where second place gets a better reward than first place."

The principal suggested, "Teacher Shen, you could appropriately reduce the first-place reward."

As principal, his thinking wasn't wrong—resources were limited and naturally should go toward cultivating students with potential.

Shen Wushuang said, "Principal, I have my own approach. If you think someone else is better suited to be the homeroom teacher, I'm willing to resign."

These words struck the principal like a thunderbolt. He hadn't expected Shen Wushuang to be so decisive.

He quickly smiled. "That's not what I meant at all. You've trained multiple special training classes over the years—I have complete confidence in your teaching ability."

"Forget it. Since you have your own approach, I won't interfere. The school's resources remain at your disposal."

Their conversation ended quickly. The principal sighed and left.

To achieve better results for this class in the next college entrance exam, the school had gone all in. As principal, he bore the greatest pressure.

If they succeeded, he would reverse Nanjiang Second High's declining fortunes. If they failed, he would become the school's villain.

"Little Star, Old Xie—do either of you have any connections? I want to exchange these for Beast Taming Pearls."

Chen Shu wanted to offload his rewards. The Beast Orbs needed to be refined, which wasted time.

As for the War Cry skill, it wasn't very useful, and the odds of comprehending it were too low anyway.

Xie Sunan's eyes went wide. "Are you crazy? Exchange them for Beast Taming Pearls?"

Basic-tier Beast Taming Pearls cost 150,000 yuan, while high-grade Beast Orbs were 120,000—but in terms of effectiveness, the latter was actually slightly better.

The only drawback was that Beast Orbs required time to refine, while Beast Taming Pearls could be directly converted into Beast Taming Power.

Chen Shu blinked and asked, "Have you ever seen me cultivate?"

"Huh?"

The two were immediately dumbfounded. They really had never seen Chen Shu cultivate or absorb any spiritual energy from heaven and earth.

At night, both of them would cultivate before sleeping, but Chen Shu just fell asleep the moment his head hit the pillow.

"Then where does your strength come from? Don't tell me it's all from consuming Beast Taming Pearls?"

Xu Xingxing's mouth twitched. Even the most outrageous rich kid wouldn't go that far.

Never cultivating from the start and relying entirely on Beast Taming Pearls? Nobody in the country played like that, right?

Chen Shu sighed and explained with an air of profound wisdom, "You don't understand. Cultivation doesn't just mean sitting in meditation. For those who truly get it—eating, sleeping, going to class, even taking a dump—it's all cultivation."

"..."

The two still looked confused. They'd never heard of taking a dump counting as cultivation.

Chen Shu sighed again and stopped explaining. *What am I supposed to say—that I've got cheats?*

**Translator's Notes:**

[1] "A sparrow pecking a cow's butt—truly awesome" (麻雀啄了牛的臀，确实牛逼): A modern internet 歇后语 (two-part saying). The pun works like this: "雀食" (que shi, "sparrow eats") sounds identical to "确实" (que shi, "indeed/really"), while "牛逼" (niu bi) is vulgar slang for "awesome." Essentially, Chen Shu is saying "I'm fucking awesome" through crude wordplay.
