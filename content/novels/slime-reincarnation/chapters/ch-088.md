Chapter 88 - How'd You Get So Experienced?
==================================================

But Fang Si remained calm and composed.


A wave of frost breath descended once more, landing directly on the Black Beetle Lord's body.


Its body was temporarily frozen.


The Scarlet Flame Dragon dove down, brilliant flames suddenly flaring on its claws as it clashed with the Black Beetle Lord once again.


Fang Si came to stand beside the Frost Dragon, observing the battle.


The Scarlet Flame Dragon was a perfectly cultivated super S-rank contract spirit with strength enough to fight the Black Beetle Lord head-on.


The two creatures' frenzied combat startled the tourists on the mountain.


They left their hotels one after another and looked toward the distant scene.


The Frost Dragon and Scarlet Flame Dragon were extremely eye-catching in the night, and combined with the occasionally exploding flames, it was like a large-scale fireworks display.


"What's that up ahead? A blue light and a red light? Could it be aliens?"


"Are you blind?! Those are flying dragons! Does Taiqing Mountain have this kind of attraction now?"


"Mom, I made it! I'm crying—I saw a dragon!"


Tourists pulled out their phones to take pictures, wanting to record the magnificent scene before them.


"I'm a park staff member! Everyone BACK TO THE HOTEL! Everyone BACK TO THE HOTEL!"


Staff members wearing work uniforms came forward with megaphones, having obviously received notification from the Spirit Suppression Bureau.


Since it was nighttime, panicking and fleeing down the mountain would definitely cause massive casualties.


Rather than take that risk, it was better to stay in the hotel and seek rescue.


"Screech!"


The Black Beetle Lord was furious beyond words. The moment it emerged from the mountain, it encountered Waterloo—just like a hundred years ago.


The Black Beetle Lord's life could be summed up in one sentence: Always attacking, always getting beaten!


Its eerie green eyes looked toward the distant hotel, and a harsh screech came from its mouth.


The swarm of Black Beetles immediately received the command, finally recovering from their spectating state and charging toward the hotel.


"Not good!"


Fang Si's expression froze. She reacted instantly—it wanted to kill ordinary people!


Roar!


The Frost Dragon roared, and an ice-blue frost breath shot toward the sky.


Dozens of Black Beetles fell in an instant, like black rain pouring down.


But skills had cooldowns. The Frost Dragon couldn't intercept the entire swarm.


Fang Si sighed, able only to pray for those people.


Stopping a lord-level fierce beast was already her limit unless she had a team helping her.


She glanced at the two guys in the tree and sighed again.


Could she possibly count on those two?


But the next moment, Chen Shu actually stood up and shouted loudly.


"Sister Fang Si, I got this!"


Fang Si's expression froze. A Golden Slime suddenly appeared.


In her astonished gaze, the slime inflated rapidly like a balloon, swelling several times larger in an instant—even bigger than the Scarlet Flame Dragon.


"This skill?"


Fang Si's eyes narrowed. She immediately realized the potential of the Gigantification skill.


The larger the slime's base body size, the more terrifying the Gigantification's amplification would be.


"Take flight!"


Chen Shu looked up at the Black Beetle swarm, a hint of a smile in his eyes.


At the critical moment, I still have to step up!


The slime's mouth puffed up at once, performing that skill combination from before again.


Wind's Breath, Charge, and Violent Sitting Kill were released in sequence.


Fang Si couldn't help but gape. Even with her extensive experience, she was completely stunned on the spot.


A giant pile of poop was actually flying?! And getting faster and faster!


Boom boom boom!


The slime's adorable big eyes looked at the ground while its body crashed into the Black Beetle swarm.


Although it couldn't see behind itself, Chen Shu wasn't blind—he served as the slime's radar.


The slime moved extremely fast, like a precision-guided missile.


The originally orderly swarm was scattered in an instant. They were just a group of impure-blooded fierce beasts—bullying ordinary people was one thing, but facing the slime, they simply weren't enough.


Those it hit were either severely injured or dead!


Countless Black Beetles crawled onto the slime's body, trying to bite it, but the skill 'Rebound Force' activated, directly shattering their mandibles.


The tourists in the hotel watched the scene in the sky, instantly dumbfounded.


With their eyesight, they couldn't make out the Black Beetles in the night—they could only see a giant piece of poop continuously soaring freely through the sky...


"Breaking news! There's poop flying in the sky!"


Everyone pulled out their phones to take pictures and post them to their social media.


Soon, the slime landed safely. The skill effects ended, and it lost its propulsion.


But the Black Beetles' formation had already been disrupted, with severe casualties.


"Chen Pi! Impressive!"


Fang Si's expression was shocked. She hadn't expected Chen Shu's F-rank slime to be this fierce.


Honestly, even an average A-rank contract spirit might not match that performance.


Chen Shu waved his hand dismissively and said, "All according to plan!"


"Screech!"


The Black Beetle Lord temporarily suppressed the Scarlet Flame Dragon. Looking at its countless dead descendants, it was furious!


"Charge!"


The Black Beetle Lord ordered the swarm to rush toward the hotel again.


The slime's skills had entered cooldown. It would have to wait for all three skills to recover before it could take flight again.


"Whether those people live or die is up to fate now."


Chen Shu was powerless. The slime could only hurt Black Beetles through its combo technique.


Fang Si stopped paying attention and focused intently on dealing with the lord-level fierce beast before her.


To kill any lord creature, one had to face a crisis—their clan!


But the Black Beetle Lord before them was an exception. Its generals from back then had long been abandoned.


Its current clan were all impure-blooded creatures—complete rabble.


And relying on its strength alone, it might not be able to defeat Fang Si.


Fang Si had the Frost Dragon join the battle. A blast of frost breath surged forth.


The Black Beetle immediately experienced the feeling of ice and fire.


"Gotta run again!"


The Black Beetle Lord felt terrible. It had just emerged from the mountain only to get beaten right back.


But the two contract spirits didn't want to let it go!


The Scarlet Flame Dragon's claws were wreathed in flames, continuously tearing at the Black Beetle Lord's shell.


As long as it didn't have that black light shield's protection, the Scarlet Flame Dragon could easily break through its defense.


Soon, the Black Beetle was severely injured.


Although both dragons also had injuries, if they kept fighting, the Black Beetle would definitely die first.


"Screech!"


The Black Beetle looked furious. Its wings spread open, black light emanating from them.


Its eerie green pupils filled with sinister coldness, and its aura suddenly became terrifying.


Fang Si frowned slightly and had the two dragons temporarily halt their attack. From the look of things, it was about to go all-out.


"Stop it quick!"


Chen Shu shouted loudly from a distance.


Fang Si's expression froze. She saw the Black Beetle, which had been preparing to flee, suddenly flap its wings and turn to run...


She immediately realized she'd been tricked. That Black Beetle had acted way too convincingly—she'd even thought it wanted to die together with its enemies.


Fang Si turned to look at Chen Shu again. How'd you get so experienced at this?!
