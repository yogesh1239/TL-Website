Chapter 112 - The Sneeze That Started It All[1]
==================================================

"Be careful! That guy's the murderer I ran into yesterday."

The three of them followed cautiously. After the man got out of the car, he headed straight toward a dark alley.

Chen Shu and his two companions exchanged glances, wicked grins spreading across their faces.

Baldy Liu, the kindergarten graduate,[2] walked into the alley.

Suddenly hearing footsteps, he took off his hat, revealing his signature shiny bald head.

He turned around, and his expression froze for a moment.

Chen Shu stood with his arms crossed, chuckling. "Yo! Isn't this Brother Liu? Haven't seen you in a few days—your head's gotten shinier!"

"So I actually hooked you lot?"

The man instantly recognized Chen Shu. If it weren't for his failed assassination attempt, he wouldn't have become a public enemy on the run.

Though he'd been wanted by the Spirit Suppression Bureau for a while now, nobody knew he was hiding out in Nanjiang City.

But now? The Nanjiang Spirit Suppression Bureau had mobilized, constantly searching for traces of him.

Baldy Liu looked at the three of them, a strange smile creeping across his face. "Kid, you came here to die?"

Chen Shu frowned, feeling a twinge of unease. *What does he mean, 'hooked us'?*

Just as he was about to speak, options appeared before his eyes.

**[Option 1: Run immediately—survival comes first! Completion reward: Large amount of Beast Taming Power]**

**[Option 2: Shout at the top of your lungs: "Help! Noooooo!"[3] Completion reward: Slime Strength +10%]**

**[Option 3: Annihilate the entire criminal gang! Completion reward: Slime All Attributes +20%]**

Chen Shu's eyes widened slightly. He ignored the rewards and locked onto the keywords: *Criminal gang!!*

*I tracked him all the way to their hideout??*

"Old Liu, is this your target?"

A voice came from the darkness at the end of the alley. Several figures slowly emerged, their faces fierce and menacing as they coldly stared at the three of them.

"Holy crap!"

Chen Shu's heart lurched. His gaze immediately shot toward Xu Xingxing.

In an instant, Xu Xingxing summoned his Mutant Thunderbird. The three of them moved with lightning speed and hopped right onto the bird.

"Think you can run?"

One of them sneered. The cement ground began to tremble, then transformed into two massive hands that locked onto the Mutant Thunderbird's claws.

Not far from the trio, a yellowish-brown humanoid creature materialized, its body shimmering with skill energy.

"An Earth Elemental Spirit! We're screwed!"

Chen Shu's heart sank. He hadn't expected to run into an organized crime ring.

Originally, he'd planned to catch the guy. Now it turned out they'd been counter-ambushed instead.

"Misunderstanding, it's all a misunderstanding..."

Chen Shu let out an awkward laugh, trying to explain while shooting Xie Sunan a look.

Almost instantaneously, a green light flashed and descended right above Baldy Liu's head, transforming into a giant green hat.[4]

Every criminal's attention was drawn to it. Their menacing eyes instantly softened with amusement.

"Old Liu, you've been cucked..."

Some of them wanted to laugh but felt it might be inappropriate. But since none of them had professional training in keeping a straight face, they all burst out laughing anyway.

"Hahaha!"

They all pointed at the hat on Baldy Liu's head. Nobody expected someone to have such a dirty skill.

Right as everyone's attention was forcibly diverted—

The Golden Slime appeared without a sound.

Chen Shu lay flat on the Slime's head, both hands gripping its nostrils.

"Get on, quick!"

Chen Shu was ready to pull his old trick again—running away was the smart play.

Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing reacted just as fast, grabbing onto the Slime's nostrils too.

They had no choice. The Slime's body was slippery as hell. Without something to grip, they'd slide right off.

"Stop laughing, you idiots! That Slime can fly!!"

Baldy Liu caught their movement from the corner of his eye.

The other criminals snapped back to reality and summoned their own contract spirits.

But by then, the Slime had already switched to flight mode.

The Earth Elemental Spirit tried to stop them, but unfortunately, the Slime had no claws. It couldn't control something that big.

"You morons! Smile for the camera!"

Chen Shu whipped out his phone, ready to photograph everyone's faces.

These guys definitely weren't good people. Perfect chance to let the Spirit Suppression Bureau sweep them all up.

Chen Shu fired off rapid shots, immortalizing the criminals' *stunning mugshots*!

Just as the three of them prepared to leave, the Slime's body began to tremble. Its nose started twitching.

The smile on Chen Shu's face froze. A bad feeling crept into his heart.

"Hold on tight!"

ACHOO!

In an instant, the Slime let out a violent sneeze.

"Crap!"

Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing cried out, instantly flung off by the tremendous force.

With six hands pinching its nostrils, anyone would want to sneeze...

"Are you kidding me?! A double reversal?!"

Chen Shu watched his friends fall, his heart clenching tight.

The Slime was fast in the air, but not very agile. It relied on wind currents to adjust direction, making it impossible to catch two people plummeting down.

Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing looked panicked on the outside, but inside they were even more freaked out...

Nobody could have predicted something like this would happen.

Fortunately, Chen Shu had been busy taking photos, so the Slime hadn't risen too high. Even if they fell, it wouldn't cause serious injury.

But the problem was—there was a gang of criminals waiting below like hungry predators!

Sure enough, the moment the two hit the ground, the criminals seized them.

"Chen Shu! Save me! Save me!"

The two looked up at Chen Shu in the sky, waving their arms frantically.

Because of the night wind, Chen Shu couldn't quite hear them.

But his eyesight was good. He could faintly read two words from their lips: "Go! Go!"

He said with a touched expression, "At a moment like this, how could I possibly leave? ...Of course, I bolted."

The Slime accelerated and vanished from everyone's sight.

Baldy Liu looked at the two captives coldly. "Looks like your friend can't save you now!"

A bespectacled young man spoke up. "Take them back first! If that guy really dares report to the Spirit Suppression Bureau, we'll kill these two."

Not far down the street, Chen Shu crept back stealthily.

He'd only pretended to leave, hoping to make them lower their guard.

His expression was now calm, the goofy demeanor gone.

These guys weren't pushovers. If he wasn't careful, Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing could very well end up dead.

"Mess with the Nanjiang Bandit's brothers? Where would that leave my reputation?"

Chen Shu arrived at the far end of the dark alley, his shrunken Golden Slime by his side.

He hadn't immediately contacted Wang Qian. If the Spirit Suppression Bureau showed up, it would tip off the gang, and his two friends would be done for.

"I'm counting on you!"

Chen Shu pulled out a potion, anticipation gleaming in his eyes.

It was the Invisibility Potion!

Last night, when he'd encountered Baldy Liu's duo, he'd chosen to flee, completing the second option's conditions and earning an Invisibility Potion.

Chen Shu summoned the Slime and fed it a small amount first, wanting to see how it worked.

Before his astonished eyes, the Slime's body vanished into thin air!

**Translator's Notes:**

[1] **The Sneeze That Started It All (一个喷嚏引发的事故)**: A play on the iconic 2005 viral video "一个馒头引发的血案" (A Murder Caused by a Steamed Bun), a parody that remixed Chen Kaige's film "The Promise." The phrase "一个XX引发的YY" (One [thing] causing [consequence]) became a popular Chinese internet template for butterfly-effect scenarios.

[2] **Kindergarten graduate (托儿所本科)**: An absurdist insult meaning someone's highest educational achievement is a "bachelor's degree from kindergarten"—implying extremely low intelligence.

[3] **"Noooooo!" (雅蠛蝶)**: Chinese phonetic spelling of the Japanese word "やめて" (yamete, meaning "stop/don't"), popularized through anime and Japanese media. Often used humorously in Chinese internet culture for exaggerated protests.

[4] **Green hat (绿帽子)**: In Chinese culture, wearing a green hat signifies being cuckolded. The association dates back to Ming Dynasty regulations requiring male relatives of prostitutes to wear green head coverings. When someone says "你绿了" (you've gone green), they mean "you've been cheated on." Xie Sunan's contract spirit literally manifesting a green hat on Baldy Liu's head is both a visual gag and a cultural burn.
