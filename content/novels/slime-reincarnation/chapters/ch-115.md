Chapter 115: I, Chen Shu, Am Duty-Bound to Serve My Classmates
=====================================

The difference in efficacy between Beast Orbs and Beast Taming Pearls could be made up with a single night's sleep anyway. After all, going to sleep always triggered an option.

One was sleeping, the other was meditating. The former gave better results—who wouldn't pick that?

"Leave it to me. My family has channels for Beast Taming Pearls."

"??" Chen Shu and Xie Sunan both turned to stare at Xu Xingxing.

"What?"

Xu Xingxing smiled sheepishly, looking a bit embarrassed.

Chen Shu quickly asked, "What does your family do?"

"Just an ordinary family. We do a bit of business." Xu Xingxing scratched his head.

Chen Shu and Xie Sunan exchanged glances, mouths twitching. *Some "ordinary family"!*

A Basic-tier Beast Taming Pearl cost 150,000 yuan. A Black Iron-tier one was 600,000. Any family in that kind of business was anything but ordinary!

"Alright, students. Let's continue distributing the rewards."

Shen Wushuang walked into the classroom and handed out all the rewards.

Chen Shu did a rough estimate—the total value of this batch of rewards was probably around five million.

"From now on, we'll hold a competition like this every month! The rewards will only increase, never decrease!"

Shen Wushuang's words instantly lit up everyone's faces with excitement.

Not everyone came from an "ordinary family." Wasn't the whole point of fighting to get into the special training class for resources like these?

With ten months until the college entrance exam, the school would be spending fifty million on competition rewards alone. Factor in other expenses, and the total probably exceeded a hundred million.

Of course, for high-ranked Beast Tamers, Huaxia currency wasn't worth as much. Beast taming resources were the real treasure.

A hundred million Huaxia yuan could only perfectly cultivate two Black Iron-rank contract spirits.

"Now we begin today's lesson. Everyone follow me to the Beast Taming Arena!"

With that, Shen Wushuang left the classroom.

Ten minutes later, the special training class arrived at the Beast Taming Arena.

"This first month, you need to learn flexible skill application!"

Shen Wushuang summoned his contract spirit. A massive python over ten meters long appeared.

Its scales were black and white, and its eyes gleamed with vicious intensity.

It was the A-rank Black-White Giant Python!

Its innate skill, Scale Conversion, was extraordinary—it could shift the color of its scales across its entire body.

Black scales had extremely high resistance to physical attacks, while white scales had extremely high resistance to elemental attacks.

Though it looked like a combat-type contract spirit, it actually leaned more toward defense—a tank, in other words.

"My Black-White Giant Python has a skill called 'Bind.' It can restrain enemies while also having a certain armor-breaking effect."

Shen Wushuang stroked the snake's head as he addressed the students below.

"Chen Shu, summon your slime."

"..." Chen Shu's mouth twitched. *So they're using my slime as a teaching prop now?*

He immediately stepped forward and said, "Classmates, I stake Young Master Tang's life on it—this snake's Bind definitely has armor-breaking effects!"

Tang Lie's mouth twitched. *What the hell is wrong with you? What does this have to do with me?*

Chen Shu then said to Shen Wushuang, "Teacher, no need for the demonstration. Everyone already gets it."

Shen Wushuang nodded, then continued, "Bind isn't just a control skill—it has other uses too. Does anyone know what they are?"

His gaze swept over the students below. Almost instinctively, every single one lowered their head.

This was another universal rule of school life!

Options appeared before Chen Shu's eyes.

[Option 1: Out of student instinct, you lower your head as well. Completion reward: Small amount of Beast Taming Power]

[Option 2: With the System, you're naturally extraordinary—so naturally, you should tilt your head up and stare at the ceiling. Completion reward: High-Grade Strength Potion]

[Option 3: Facing Shen Wushuang's scrutinizing gaze, you decide to meet it with an equally scrutinizing stare. Completion reward: Berserk Potion]

Chen Shu stroked his chin, then suddenly raised his head and locked eyes with Shen Wushuang.

Having experienced the benefits of the Invisibility Potion, there was no way he'd pass up the Berserk Potion. It might just turn the tide in a crisis.

Shen Wushuang was thinking about whom to call on when Chen Shu suddenly raised his head.

That gaze was even more scrutinizing than his own.

For a moment, Shen Wushuang felt like he'd been transported back to his student days and instinctively looked away.

His mouth twitched, and he quickly recovered his teacherly authority.

*What kind of look was that? He made ME feel insecure...*

He pointed at Chen Shu. "Chen Pi, you answer."

"Reward obtained: Berserk Potion!"

Chen Shu happily collected his reward and began speaking. "Bind is extremely fast to execute. Not only can it quickly restrain enemies, but it's also a protective skill."

"Oh? Go on." Shen Wushuang nodded slightly.

"If the host is in danger, the Black-White Giant Python can rapidly coil around them, blocking incoming attacks."

Shen Wushuang's eyes showed approval. He had used this exact technique to save his own life in an Alternate Dimension.

The Black-White Giant Python's natural speed wasn't fast enough for quick protection, but with the skill's boost, its speed would skyrocket.

It was like how the slime's natural jumping ability was weak, but with Violent Sitting Kill's enhancement, it could leap dozens of meters in a single bound.

Just as Shen Wushuang was about to speak, he raised an eyebrow. Chen Shu was still going, speaking with casual confidence.

"The third use is to rapidly coil its body to reduce its size and dodge enemy attacks."

Chen Shu's expression remained calm as he continued, "Of course, no skill's application is set in stone. You need to adapt based on the specific situation."

Shen Wushuang studied Chen Shu carefully. "Where did you learn all this? Did you already know about Bind before?"

"Nope." Chen Shu spread his hands innocently. "Don't you just need a brain for this?"

"..."

The students below all twitched. *So apparently none of us have brains.*

"Chen Shu is right. No skill has a fixed application. An offensive skill might be a defensive skill, or it might be a control skill. It all depends on how you use it."

Shen Wushuang nodded in agreement. Chen Shu's understanding of skills was clearly a level above everyone else's.

"Xu Xiaoyu, summon your contract spirit!"

A fiery bird appeared, continuously radiating scorching heat.

Tang Lie beside her instinctively stepped back. Clearly, he was still traumatized.

Chen Shu crossed his arms. "Young Master Tang, did you seriously just take half a step back?"

Just as the two were about to start bickering, Shen Wushuang spoke up. "Alright, Chen Shu. You'll explain skill flexibility to your classmates, starting with Xu Xiaoyu."

Chen Shu said flatly, "Mr. Shen, I'm just a student, you know?"

*This is basically using me as free labor. There are over twenty students in this class—how long is this going to take?*

"From now on, you're the special training class's Class Monitor!"

"Nope!" Before any other student could speak, Chen Shu rejected it first. "Absolutely zero interest!"

"Two High-Grade Beast Orbs per month!"

"To serve my classmates, I, Chen Shu, am duty-bound![1] Anyone who tries to take this from me will have ME to answer to!"

---

**Translator's Notes:**

[1] "To serve my classmates" (为同学服务): A parody of Mao Zedong's famous 1944 slogan "为人民服务" (Serve the People), which became the official motto of the People's Liberation Army. Chen Shu uses grandiose revolutionary rhetoric for trivial favors, creating a comedic mismatch between form and content.
