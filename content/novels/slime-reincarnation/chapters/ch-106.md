Chapter 106: Whoa Whoa Whoa! What's With That Getup?

Chen Shu paused for a moment, his eyes deep in thought.

If he followed his heart, he'd absolutely have Xie Sunan give Tang Lie a little taste of love's radiant light.

But contract spirits couldn't be summoned freely in the cafeteria—that would break the rules.

He sighed. Looked like he'd have to settle for beating the crap out of Tang Lie the old-fashioned way...

Chen Shu smiled slightly. "I don't know if I'll be tough this afternoon, but you're about to find out you're not so tough yourself!"

"What? You want to throw hands?!"

Tang Lie narrowed his eyes, looking completely composed.

Du Long and his two lackeys closed in, and the four other guys from the special training class walked over too—clearly all on the same team.

"Think you've got numbers on me?"

Chen Shu laughed. He hadn't expected Tang Lie to come prepared.

Tang Lie smiled. "That's right. I heard you're quite the fighter."

Just as everyone was about to make their move—

Chen Shu struck first!

With a strange smile, he instantly charged at Tang Lie.

Tang Lie instinctively raised his arm to block, and Chen Shu practically flew backward in the same instant, crashing to the ground.

"Huh? Did my body get enhanced or something?"

Tang Lie's eyes went wide. He hadn't even used any force.

Chen Shu clutched his chest, his face a mask of agony and tragic heroism as he bellowed: "Tang Lie! How dare you insult the Regular Class!!"

"???"

Tang Lie was completely baffled. When did I insult the Regular Class?

Sure, he looked down on regular students all the time, but he hadn't said anything today, had he?

The regular students eating in the cafeteria froze, then turned to stare at Tang Lie in unison.

Tang Lie's heart sank. He instantly realized what Chen Shu was doing.

*You sneaky bastard!*

Tang Lie tried to explain, but unfortunately, his voice was nowhere near as loud as Chen Shu's.

"You're still calling regular students trash?! I'll fight you to the death!"

Chen Shu charged at Tang Lie again.

Sure enough, his body "flew" back several meters.

"Still talking?! You're still fucking talking?! I can't take it anymore!"

"Today I'll put my life on the line to defend the Regular Class's dignity!"

"AHHH!!"

Chen Shu kept shouting, his body flying through the air again and again, looking absolutely brutal.

Tang Lie and his crew stood there slack-jawed, watching Chen Shu's passionate performance.

*Dude, your acting is way too over the top...*

In an instant, regular students throughout the cafeteria rose to their feet and surrounded Tang Lie.

Looking at the dozens of people around them, the eight guys from the special training class were stunned.

*You rallied this many people with just a few sentences?*

Seeing things going south, Lin Xingchen from the special training class reacted lightning-fast.

He immediately roared at Tang Lie: "Who do you think you are?! Looking down on regular students?! Beat his ass!"

With that, he threw the first punch right at Tang Lie!

*This teamfight is going great!*

Even Chen Shu hadn't expected such a decisive backstabber...

Once one person started, the rest was inevitable. Mob mentality would always exist.

Du Long's face went pale too. He hadn't expected things to escalate this far.

"Oh right, I think I forgot to get my food."

With that, Du Long executed a smooth pivot and casually slipped out of the crowd.

In the end, only Hong Ming and Wang Yun remained loyal, getting beaten alongside Tang Lie.

The two of them wailed miserably: "Stop hitting us! Please stop hitting us..."

Chen Shu, meanwhile, made his exit after accomplishing his mission, strolling out of the cafeteria with Xu Xingxing.

Xie Sunan looked on in admiration. "Chen Shu, you're really something else!"

Chen Shu smiled slightly. "You think this was my doing?"

"Wasn't it?"

"The real reason is that Tang Lie has always mocked regular students. They didn't dare fight back before, but as soon as they found an opening, they naturally went all out."

The regular students weren't idiots either. Many of them saw through Chen Shu's act—the plot was way too ridiculous.

This was Chen Shu, after all...

Would he really sacrifice his own interests for the group's honor?

But real or fake, nobody cared. As long as they got to beat the crap out of Tang Lie, that was enough.

Xu Xingxing nodded in agreement. "This is what they call karma—it's not that it won't come, the time just wasn't right!"

The three returned to their dorm and enjoyed a nice afternoon nap.

The cafeteria chaos lasted a full half hour.

Even Chen Shu hadn't expected things to spiral this far out of control.

The moment anyone heard Tang Lie was the one getting beaten, their eyes lit up with excitement.

Clearly, the guy had made plenty of enemies!

When afternoon classes began, the three of them headed to the Beast Taming Arena.

Shen Wushuang looked over the class, a hint of confusion in his eyes. How were they already missing three more people?

He pulled out the attendance list, about to call roll, when the arena doors suddenly swung open.

The students all turned to look—and immediately burst out laughing.

There sat Tang Lie in a wheelchair, right leg wrapped in bandages, eyes blazing with fury.

Beside him, Wang Yun had his left arm bandaged, pushing the wheelchair with his right hand.

Hong Ming was the opposite—pushing the wheelchair with his left hand.

The three of them looked like the poster children for the sick and injured as they entered the arena.

Chen Shu couldn't help but smirk as he called out: "Whoa whoa whoa whoa whoa! What's with that getup? Pretty stylish! Very 6+7, huh?"[1]

The whole class erupted in laughter.

Tang Lie's mouth twitched, rage boiling inside him. "You're done, Chen Shu! You are absolutely done!"

Shen Wushuang was equally baffled. They'd been perfectly fine this morning—how'd they end up like this by afternoon?

"Alright! Alright! Let the matches begin!"

Shen Wushuang shook his head, not too concerned.

Though Tang Lie's injuries looked serious, medical technology in this era was extremely advanced. He'd be healed in two or three days at most. He'd just have to make do with the wheelchair for today.

"First match: Chen Shu versus Du Long!"

Starting off with the main event!

The tournament had now reached the Top 6 to Top 3 stage. Only Xu Xiaoyu, Wang Qinghan, Du Long, Tang Lie, Xu Xingxing, and Chen Shu remained.

Everyone turned to watch, eager to see the clash between an F-rank Slime and an A-rank Sub-dragon!

Du Long said coldly: "Looks like your luck isn't great!"

Chen Shu sighed. "Little Dragon Man,[2] you really shouldn't stick your nose into this. The waters run deep here. Take my advice—you really can't handle it!"

"Cut the crap!"

Du Long summoned his contract spirit—the A-rank Earth Sub-dragon!

A sub-dragon covered in gray scales appeared on the arena stage.

Both its presence and appearance radiated absolute dominance.

An oppressive aura instantly swept across the entire venue!

Du Long sneered. "Let me see this legendary slime of yours."

"You really want to see it?"

Chen Shu raised an eyebrow, a mysterious smile playing at the corners of his lips.

Du Long spoke up. "What, thinking of surrendering?"

He suddenly turned his back to the arena, facing the rest of the class with a look of superiority.

"Trying to compete with my sub-dragon using a slime? That's nothing but a pipe dream. A sub-dragon with true dragon bloodline is known as the undefeatable at the same rank!"

Du Long grew more and more animated, spreading his arms wide. "I'm not targeting anyone in particular...!"[3]

*BOOM!*

In that instant, the ground began to tremble.

Everyone's jaws dropped, their expressions frozen in shock.

Even Shen Wushuang's eyes contracted as he murmured: "What a waste of heavenly talent! An F-rank slime actually learned Gigantification!"

**Translator's Notes:**
[1] "Very 6+7" (非常6+7) is a play on "非常6+1" (Very 6+1), a famous CCTV variety show (2003-present) where contestants undergo 6 days of makeover training before appearing on day 7. Chen Shu mockingly suggests their beating was quite the "transformation."
[2] "Little Dragon Man" (小龙人) references a beloved 1992 Chinese children's TV mascot—a cute boy in a dragon costume. Chen Shu uses it to infantilize Du Long's dragon pride.
[3] "I'm not targeting anyone specifically..." is a famous line from Stephen Chow's film "King of Destruction" (破坏之王, 1994). The full quote continues: "I'm saying everyone here is trash." Du Long gets interrupted before completing the insult.
