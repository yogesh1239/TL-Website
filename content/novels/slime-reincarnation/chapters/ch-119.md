Chapter One Hundred Nineteen: Want Invincible Pets? Give 'Em Strength Potion!
================================================================================

"I was wrong! No mental issues, none at all!"

Chen Shu collapsed onto the soft earth, feeling the dead silence around him. A chill ran through him.

This was straight out of a murder mystery—dark night, high winds, not a soul around.

He had no choice but to come clean, telling Shen Wushuang everything about the Spirit Suppression Bureau.

"You actually arrested criminals?"

Shen Wushuang looked utterly skeptical. "You didn't just sell out your own accomplices, did you?"

"..." Chen Shu was speechless.

Damn, you really dare to think that? Do I look like a criminal?

In the end, Shen Wushuang reluctantly believed him. Of course, as punishment, Chen Shu still spent half an hour in the dirt pit...

"Finally back."

Returning to the dorm, Chen Shu felt a wave of emotion. Now he truly understood how precious life was.

Xie Sunan asked, "Chen Pi, why are you covered in dirt?"

"You know nothing! I was communing with nature!"

Chen Shu stripped off his clothes, preparing to take a shower. "And you two—why didn't you warn me about the danger?"

"Who would dare? That snake was terrifying. You didn't see it—before you got back, it was sharpening its teeth the whole time..."

Both of them clearly had their own trauma. No wonder they'd looked like that when Chen Shu returned.

...

Early the next morning.

Chen Shu obediently showed up at the Beast Taming Arena.

He couldn't help but miss the good old days. Back when he was in Beast Taming Class 5, absolutely no one managed him—complete freedom.

"You really are something ruthless!"

Chen Shu looked up at Shen Wushuang on the platform.

He made up his mind: someday, he'd bury that Black-White Giant Python for ten days... no, fifteen days.

"Chen Pi, come up here and supervise!"

"Coming..." Chen Shu instantly switched to a smile and climbed onto the platform.

Shen Wushuang said seriously, "Today's task is still skill practice. Don't think subjects that aren't on the college entrance exam don't matter—this is all about improving your real strength."

"Ohhh~" The students below responded weakly.

Clearly, skill practice was unbearably boring.

Shen Wushuang frowned. After a moment, he said, "The fundamental reason Chen Pi can beat all of you is that he knows how to use skills. If you practice diligently, beating him up isn't just a dream!"

Instantly, everyone in class got fired up like they'd been injected with adrenaline, reviving to full health.

"..."

Chen Shu's mouth twitched. Damn, is this really necessary?

Also—Xie Sunan, what the hell are you working so hard for?!

With a single sentence from Shen Wushuang, the whole class found their motivation.

Originally, their ideals might have been things like getting into university or becoming legendary Beast Tamers.

But now, they only wanted one thing: to beat the crap out of Chen Shu...

"In that case, let me help you work even harder."

Chen Shu grinned, hands behind his back as he pointed out the other students' mistakes.

"Hey, Du Long! Do you even know how to use Earth Spike and Ground Wave? Your Earth Sub-dragon's turned into Earth Sub-worm!"

"Young Master Tang, your Wind Runner Beast still hasn't been rerolled? Stop holding on!"

"Wang Yun, what the hell is your Desert Crocodile doing? That Tail Whip talent is so pathetic it should go perform at the senior citizens' square, spinning tops with the old folks."

Chen Shu wore a bratty smirk, mercilessly crushing everyone's spirits.

"If you want to surpass me, Chen Shu, I'd say your odds are about the same as becoming Nation-Guarding Beast Tamers."

The entire group worked even harder in the Beast Taming Arena, earnestly practicing their skill usage. This was a stage every strong fighter had to go through.

Chen Shu also summoned his slime to practice skills and improve its obedience.

High obedience was like having low ping in a game—who would turn that down?

...

Several days passed in a flash.

During the day, Chen Shu mocked his classmates at school while practicing skills. At night, he handled all the paperwork for his black market shop.

Weekend evening.

Chen Shu didn't go home. Instead, he went with Wang Tianbao and the others to the black market shop.

"Today we're celebrating—Summer Star Potion Company has gone bankrupt!"

Chen Shu was all smiles. He'd gotten word from Wang Qian yesterday.

Before the other companies could react, it was time to open the shop.

Chen Shu said passionately, "Also, today is the official grand opening of our Strength Potion Shop!"

Clap clap clap!

Wang Tianbao and the other six applauded, but the atmosphere felt strangely muted.

Wang Tianbao suddenly said, "Young Master Chen, I think there's a problem."

"What problem?"

"Speaking logically, as a potion shop, we should at least have... potions..."

Everyone stood in a completely empty shop. Inside was one shelf, one table, a few chairs—and absolutely nothing else...

"What's the rush?"

Chen Shu said calmly, "Wait here a sec!"

Then Chen Shu bolted straight for the black market's public toilets.

"..."

The six of them stared wide-eyed, looking at each other. They wanted to say something but didn't know how to put it into words.

After a long pause, Wang Tianbao said, "Young Master Chen is going to the bathroom... not sourcing product... right?"

"Uh... probably not..."

The six exchanged glances, genuinely starting to doubt.

Chen Shu arrived at the familiar stall in the public toilet.

He remembered the first time he'd brewed potions here—someone photographed him and it even made it onto the internet.

Several months later, the legend of the Public Toilet Potion Master still lived on in Nanjiang City...

Chen Shu set out his various materials. The main ingredients were four Power Flowers—buying these materials had completely wiped him out.

Yesterday his wallet accidentally got lost, but the police returned it to him, telling him: "Everything else is here, just the money's gone."

Chen Shu had looked utterly despondent. He didn't dare admit the truth—the money had never been stolen...

"It all depends on you now!"

Chen Shu entered auto-brew mode.

Crafting High-Grade Strength Potions was quite difficult. After an hour, Chen Shu successfully obtained ten bottles of High-Grade Strength Potion.

Chen Shu cheerfully carried the ten bottles of yellow liquid back to the shop.

Wang Tianbao and the others practically jumped backward on instinct, eyes filled with horror. "Young Master Chen, I think this is... inappropriate!"

"What's inappropriate?"

"All my permits are in order. Of course I should be selling potions."

"Are you sure that's potion and not... urine?"

Everyone's mouths twitched. They couldn't help but doubt—after all, Chen Shu had just come from the public toilet!

"Nonsense! This is absolutely genuine High-Grade Strength Potion!"

Chen Shu placed the ten bottles on the only shelf. The shop looked rather empty, but that was the charm!

"We're only selling ten bottles a day! A little hunger marketing!"

He put up the price tags: only 28,888 yuan per bottle of High-Grade Strength Potion!

Market price for High-Grade Strength Potions was 30,000 yuan. Chen Shu naturally made it a bit cheaper—how else would he attract customers?

Selling a limited ten bottles per day wouldn't affect the broader Strength Potion market either.

High-Grade Strength Potions cost about 150,000 yuan to make, while revenue was close to 300,000—a pure profit of over 100,000 per day.

Chen Shu only needed to brew once daily to earn over three million Huaxia currency per month—equivalent to a Lord-tier fierce beast.

"Alright! You guys go outside and start hawking! Shout what's written on this paper!"

Chen Shu handed the six of them a paper with promotional slogans.

"Young Master Chen, do we really have to shout this?"

"Of course you have to shout it!"

Wang Tianbao and the others went outside the shop, each grabbing a megaphone and starting to hawk.

"Want invincible pets? Give 'em Strength Potion!"

"One bottle: refreshes the mind! Two bottles: never get tired! Three bottles: eternal life! Strength Potion—tastes amazing!"

"When I grow up, I'll hunt fierce beasts in alternate dimensions—Master gives me my favorite Strength Potion! I'll become the strongest contract spirit—Master gives me my favorite Strength Potion!"
