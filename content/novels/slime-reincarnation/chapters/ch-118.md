Chapter 118: Today's an Auspicious Day—Time for a Burial

=====================================

Before long, the shop owner arrived.

After some negotiation, the two parties settled on a monthly rent of 150,000 yuan.

The black market had considerable foot traffic—practically a second downtown for Nanjiang City. Combined with the fact that all the shops here dealt exclusively in beast taming merchandise, naturally the rent was steep.

Just three months' rent totaled 450,000 yuan, which put a serious dent in Chen Shu's balance.

"Hey, little boss, you hiring staff?"

The former clerk approached with an ingratiating smile. With the shop leased out, he was naturally out of a job.

"Sure am. Same work as before—400 yuan a month, probation period pays half, and don't worry, probation's not long... just one year."

The man's mouth twitched. He silently turned and walked away. *So you never saw me as a human being to begin with, huh?*

Wang Tianbao asked, "Young Master Chen, are you really going to hire someone?"

"Of course not. We've already got enough people for a shop this small."

Chen Shu directed everyone to tidy up the place, then headed to the Beast Tamer Association.

He posted another purchasing task for Power Flowers—still 30,000 yuan per stem, but now limited to just four per day.

After the Summer Star incident, Chen Shu didn't dare disrupt the market on a large scale anymore. On one hand, relevant government departments might want to "have a chat" with him; on the other, competitors would have their eyes on him.

Four Power Flowers per day wouldn't make any waves. He planned to sell just ten bottles of High-Grade Strength Potion daily.

He'd make money, save time, and stay out of danger.

Chen Shu then took Wang Tianbao and the others to a regular medicine shop to stock up on ingredients for High-Grade Strength Potions.

The main purpose was to familiarize everyone with the process. From now on, Chen Shu only needed to brew once a day, and he'd have a steady stream of Huaxia currency flowing in every month.

After bustling around all day, Wang Tianbao and the others had the routine down.

"Our shop won't open for a while yet. Everyone just rest up for now."

With Summer Star Potion Company still standing, he needed to keep a low profile.

That evening, Chen Shu returned to school. He grabbed a quick bite at the cafeteria and headed back to his dorm.

"What's up? Don't recognize me anymore?"

Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing were both staring at him with strange expressions, like they wanted to say something.

After a moment, Xie Sunan spoke. "Chen Pi, you pulled a real 'act first, ask later' move today. Teacher Shen's pretty pissed."

Chen Shu couldn't have cared less. "The class monitor thing? What business does some lowly homeroom teacher have sticking his nose into that?"

Xu Xingxing chimed in, "Chen Pi, you're not scared at all?"

"Obviously not. I'm the goddamn Nanjiang Bandit—you think I'd be scared of some small-time teacher?"

Chen Shu waved his hand dismissively, completely unconcerned.

Xu Xingxing's mouth twitched. There was already a hint of fear in his eyes as he said, "Chen Pi... doesn't your scalp feel a little cold?"

"Cold?"

Chen Shu didn't understand what he meant. He touched his head and—sure enough, it felt kind of damp.

"Is the dorm leaking? Wait, this is the ground floor..."

Chen Shu frowned and looked up at the ceiling.

In that instant, a ferocious serpent head was staring down at him with savage intensity. Drool dripped from its mouth, and the corners of its lips even curved into a disturbingly human-like evil grin.

"HOLY MOTHER OF—!"

Chen Shu's cocky expression froze solid. Almost instantaneously, he summoned his slime.

Moving at lightning speed, he leaped onto the slime's head and jammed his fingers into its nostrils.

"Take off! NOW!"

The slime's mouth whooshed with howling wind as it activated their combination skill.

But just as they were about to fly off the balcony, the black-and-white python used "Bind," snatching the slime right out of midair.

CRASH!

Chen Shu and his contract spirit slammed into the dorm room floor together.

"Isn't this the Nanjiang Bandit himself?"

Shen Wushuang strolled in from outside the room with a faint smile on his lips.

As Chen Shu lay sprawled on the ground, system options immediately materialized before his eyes.

[Option 1: Cry and apologize—a real man knows when to bend! Reward: Medium Beast Taming Power]

[Option 2: Shout angrily: "You sneak attacked! You're a sore loser! You little piece of trash!" Reward: Death Potion]

[Option 3: Fake being seriously injured to win sympathy. Reward: Leap Potion Recipe]

Chen Shu lay on the floor, mind racing, and decided on Option 3.

Though he wanted to pick Option 2... he had a feeling that "Death Potion" reward was hinting at something...

He stayed on the ground, motionless.

"Hm? Chen Pi?"

Shen Wushuang frowned and approached.

*This can't be right. This kid's body is tougher than anyone's...*

"Well, since it's come to this... let's just bury him."

Chen Shu's heart lurched. *You're really that decisive, aren't you?!*

The black-and-white python coiled around his body and carried him out of the dorm, heading straight for the hills behind the school.

Just as the python started digging a grave, Chen Shu's body suddenly convulsed.

He cracked his eyes open and began coughing violently.

"Cough... cough... Teacher, I think... I can still be saved..."

Shen Wushuang crossed his arms and smiled coolly. "I don't care what you think—I care what I think! Today's an auspicious day, so we're burying you!"

"What the f—!"

Chen Shu's mouth twitched. *So you need to pick an auspicious date just to dig a grave and bury someone now?*

"Teacher, I'm fine. Really, I'm fine..."

Chen Shu struggled to his feet. But mid-sentence, he suddenly spewed a huge mouthful of blood.

He happened to have one of Wang Tianbao's new blood packets on him. Wang Tianbao had sworn the effect was remarkable and insisted Chen Shu try it out.

*Let's see if this freaks you out!*

"Hm?"

Shen Wushuang's expression grew serious. He walked over to Chen Shu and gave a sniff.

A smile immediately spread across his face. "Dig! Keep digging! We're absolutely burying him today!"

"..."

Chen Shu's head was covered in black lines. *You figured it out that fast? Could you at least show some respect for Wang Tianbao's research?!*

"Reward obtained: Leap Potion Recipe unlocked!"

Though he'd been seen through immediately, the instant Chen Shu spat blood, it had still triggered a moment of sympathy from Shen Wushuang.

"Teacher, I was wrong."

Chen Shu watched the pit beside him grow deeper and deeper. A night breeze blew past, and a chill crept into his heart.

*You're a python! How are you so good at digging graves?!*

Chen Shu secretly sized up Shen Wushuang. *How many people have you buried already?*

Shen Wushuang spoke with a faint smile. "Oh, you know you were wrong? Was that a notice or a discussion?"

"A notice! I said I was wrong, so I was wrong!!"

Chen Shu's tone was fierce—using the toughest words to say the most cowardly things.

Shen Wushuang asked, "Come clean. Where were you today?"

"At the hospital. Really, I was seeing a doctor."

"With a body like yours, you're sick? What, mentally ill?"

"No way! How could I have any mental problems? The doctor said my condition is extremely rare and requires long-term treatment."

A flicker of interest crossed Shen Wushuang's face. "Oh? What exactly is this condition?"

"Uh... viral side stitch."

"Dig! Keep digging!"

Shen Wushuang's mouth twitched. *This kid really knows how to bullshit—'viral side stitch,' seriously?*
