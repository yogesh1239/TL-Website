Chapter 76: This is Beep~~, Not Beep!

Bang! Bang! Bang!

Fang Si's punches hit flesh, each one terrifyingly powerful. Just the sound alone was enough to make your scalp go numb.

"You?!"

The other five guys sobered up a bit, glaring over in fury.

"What? Want to help him?!"

Fang Si smiled viciously, completely unconcerned about the five of them.

In her rage, Fang Si was like a lioness—just the powerful aura she radiated was enough to send chills down your spine.

The other five clenched their fists, but they just couldn't bring themselves to step forward and stop her.

"Fang Si! You're asking for death!"

The guy trapped under the bag spat out venomous words, not expecting her to be this arrogant.

In an instant, the space above his head suddenly tore open.

A ferocious snake head emerged, and the moment it appeared, a sinister chill filled the air.

As a genius of Huaxia Academy, his control over his Beast Space naturally far surpassed ordinary people.

Summoning part of a contract spirit's body was called Partial Summoning—something all first-year students had to master.

Fang Si sneered coldly as the space above her head also shattered.

A massive dragon head appeared, its red scales vivid under the neon lights, with flames clinging to them.

This was Fang Si's first S-rank contract spirit: the Scarlet Flame Pseudo-Dragon!

Or more accurately, the Scarlet Flame Dragon. When Fang Si became a Black Iron rank Beast Tamer, it had undergone a bloodline evolution and shed the "Pseudo" designation.

Though it hadn't reached the terrifying SS-rank, it was already far more powerful than S-rank.

"ROAR!"

The Scarlet Flame Dragon bellowed and suddenly bit down hard on the snake head.

The two fearsome contract spirits constantly tore at each other, fighting, yet the aftershocks of battle were controlled within a five-meter radius and didn't even affect the street.

This was Huaxia Academy's first rule: Students were allowed to summon contract spirits in the city, but they couldn't harm unrelated people. Otherwise, they'd face extremely severe punishment.

Even though Bai Yuan was drunk, he still instinctively controlled his contract spirit, not daring to affect ordinary people.

Soon, his Dark Crystal Snake fell into a disadvantage, getting absolutely wrecked.

The man himself was also getting pummeled, his originally handsome face now swollen like a pig's head.

The Dark Crystal Snake finally couldn't take it anymore and returned to its Beast Space.

Fang Si likewise recalled the Scarlet Flame Dragon and kicked Bai Yuan—still wearing the fertilizer bag—flying several meters. He looked utterly ridiculous.

"Scram! You worthless bottom-feeders!"

Fang Si dusted off her hands, her domineering appearance drawing stares from passersby.

The remaining five barely dared to make eye contact with her, hurriedly dragging Bai Yuan away from the scene.

That's Sister Fang for you—savage and unhinged!

Chen Shu and Zhang Dali calmly continued eating their meal, with no intention of helping whatsoever.

The two of them might fail at showing off, but they'd never seen Fang Si lose a fight.

Zhang Dali swallowed and asked, "Sister Fang Si, that was your classmate?"

"Yeah. He probably couldn't stand me normally, and now alcohol's given this coward some nerve to provoke me."

Fang Si nodded and continued eating, not taking it to heart at all.

Plenty of people couldn't stand her. Who the hell was Bai Yuan anyway?

Though Huaxia Academy was the highest-level Beast Taming university, the internal competition was extremely brutal. Aside from your own Beast Taming squad, basically everyone else was an enemy.

The total amount of resources was fixed. If you didn't fight for them, someone else would take them.

An hour later, the three patted their full bellies.

Don't let Fang Si's tall, lean figure fool you—her appetite was no less than Chen Shu's and Zhang Dali's.

"Sister Xiao, check please!"

The hotel Chen Shu had booked was nearby. After sitting on a train all day, they were tired and ready to head back to rest.

They agreed that tomorrow morning, Fang Si would show them around Huaxia Academy.

Chen Shu got back to the hotel and immediately crashed, falling into a deep sleep.

At Huaxia Academy's medical office, Bai Yuan—beaten into a pig's head—kept wailing. The drunkenness had faded, and the pain felt like it had doubled.

The school doctor applied medicinal wine to Bai Yuan's face. "Who hit you this hard? This is a bit much."

One of the guys who'd come with Bai Yuan said, "Who else? Fang Si!"

"Oh, then it's fine." The doctor nodded with understanding. "Looks like Fang Si was in a good mood today—she went easy on him."

"..." The five were speechless. Talk about double standards.

Lady, you JUST asked who hit him this hard...

Bai Yuan wailed, "Liu Ziyi, why the hell didn't you guys help me? Fuck!"

He'd thought there were six of them, but in the end, he'd still fought alone. So much for brotherhood.

Liu Ziyi sighed. "Boss, you know how powerful Fang Si's two contract spirits are. How could we win?"

Another guy suddenly said with a serious expression, "I think it's seventy-thirty."

"See? Wang Qi is way more confident than you cowards!"

"Uh, Boss... when I said seventy-thirty, I meant we'd last three minutes and Fang Si's two contract spirits would be seventy percent full..."

"Get lost!"

Bai Yuan roared, accidentally pulling his wounds, making him howl in pain.

"Boss, seriously, stop provoking Fang Si." Someone sighed. "We're about to be juniors. The seniors are basically all hunting in Different Spaces—there's really no one left at school who can control her anymore."

Bai Yuan said, "You think I wanted to? I was hammered! Why didn't you stop me?!"

"We did try to stop you! You were the one who wanted to dig your own grave..."

...

Early the next morning, Chen Shu and Zhang Dali arrived at the gates of Huaxia Academy.

Two stone dragons stood with solemn expressions, looking extremely imposing.

"No wonder they're geniuses carefully selected from across the nation."

Chen Shu observed the students coming and going. Just from their bearing alone, you could tell they were exceptional—not a mediocre one among them.

"Come on, let's go check it out."

The two approached the entrance. The male student ahead of them took out a card and swiped it against a device at the gate.

Beep!

The gate opened and the student walked in.

"Looks like you need to swipe a card." Zhang Dali leaned over and whispered.

Weren't universities supposed to allow free entry and exit? Huaxia Academy actually had such strict control?

Just then, options appeared before Chen Shu's eyes.

**[Option 1: Wait at the entrance for Fang Si to come out]**
- **Reward**: Small Beast Taming Power boost

**[Option 2: Try to sneak into Huaxia Academy]**
- **Reward**: Slime Defense +5%

**[Option 3: Force your way in]**
- **Reward**: Obtain title "Fearless Hero"; equipped effect: Slime's personality becomes brave

Chen Shu immediately passed on the third option. Never mind the teachers—any random student at Huaxia Academy could beat him to death.

Looked like he could only choose the second option.

Chen Shu put on a calm expression and pretended to pull out his ID card.

"Beep!"

The gate had no reaction whatsoever.

"Hey mister, I think my card's broken. Can you open the gate for me?"

The guard's mouth twitched. "You really think I've got dementia?"

"Could you not make the sound yourself? And could you at least imitate it better? This is beep~~, not beep!"

The security guard looked exasperated. What kind of clown just wandered over here?

"Uh... sorry!"

Chen Shu looked embarrassed and pulled out his ID card again.

"Beep~~"

You had to admit, the imitation was pretty spot-on...

"Where's my taser? Where's my taser?" The security guard started patting himself down.
