Chapter 120: Walking Right Into the Cannon's Fire
=====================================

The bewitching hawking cries echoed through the black market.

Passersby stopped in their tracks one after another, turning their gazes toward the Strength Potion Shop.

Outside the shop stood a wooden sign that read: High-Grade Strength Potion! Only 28,888 yuan per bottle! Limited to ten bottles per day!

In an instant, quite a few people came over.

Someone spoke up, "Is this really a High-Grade Strength Potion?"

Chen Shu called out, "Absolutely genuine! If it's fake, I'll pay ten times over! Ten times over!"

"You're not just going to give me eleven fake bottles, are you?"

"Oh, give me a break! Absolutely genuine, personally guaranteed by Wang Qian of the Spirit Suppression Bureau!"

Chen Shu had once again roped Wang Qian in as his free brand ambassador. If anyone got a fake, they could report it directly to the Spirit Suppression Bureau.

Besides, he had an actual shop—way more credible than a ground stall!

However, what Chen Shu hadn't expected was that even though everyone believed him, there still weren't many people rushing to buy.

There was only one reason: no money!

To consume High-Grade Strength Potions up to the absorption limit required forty bottles—over a million yuan. The average person simply couldn't afford it.

And those who could afford it—would they really care about saving a thousand or so yuan?

They'd rather buy from legitimate stores in the city. At least those were guaranteed authentic!

Over the course of one whole night, Chen Shu only sold one bottle...

"Damn, I miscalculated..."

Chen Shu had assumed they'd fly off the shelves easily. Now it seemed he'd been a bit naive.

"Tomorrow, drop the price. Twenty-five thousand per bottle! Limit five bottles!"

Chen Shu pulled out a new wooden sign and scrawled two lines on it. The handwriting was sloppy, but people could still make out what it said.

"Today, I've decided to make a decision that dishonors my ancestors: High-Grade Strength Potion, 25,000 yuan each, limit 5 bottles!"[1]

"You ask why the limit? Obviously I'm afraid my ancestors will come haunt me at midnight!"

Chen Shu nodded with satisfaction. That should attract some customers.

He was just a small shop—no way he could compete with those established century-old stores. His only option was a price war.

Five bottles of potion flowing into the market each day shouldn't cause any major disruption.

"This'll do for now. Once I build up the shop's reputation, I can increase the quantity and raise prices!"

First attract customers with discounts, then jack up the prices—standard business practice...

Nanjiang Second High, Dorm 102.

Xie Sunan said, "Chen Pi, what have you been doing every night lately? You always come back so tired. Don't tell me you're out there doing..."

"Obviously I'm going out to do..." Chen Shu paused for effect, then continued, "...money!"

"..." Xu Xingxing advised, "It's senior year now. Your beast taming results are what matter most."

Chen Shu pursed his lips. "Don't talk to me about that bullshit beast taming power. Right now, I only want to make money!"

With that, Chen Shu washed up and immediately collapsed into bed.

Xie Sunan sighed. "Man, Chen Pi's wasting his talent."

Xu Xingxing let out a breath. "Yeah. At this rate, I feel like within a month, basically everyone in the class will have surpassed him."

"Looks like we're the only ones with both talent AND dedication."

The two shared a knowing smile and prepared to begin cultivating their beast taming power.

Just then, a sudden aura emerged from the sleeping Chen Shu, making his entire being seem subtly different.

"Huh?"

The two of them widened their eyes, faces full of disbelief.

Xu Xingxing swallowed hard. "Old Xie, why does it feel like he just... broke through?"

Xie Sunan wore an equally ghost-seeing expression. "Seems like it..."

Chen Shu was already a Level 7 Beast Tamer. Breaking through again would make him Level 8—officially the undisputed number one in the special training class.

"But... but he's SLEEPING?!"

The shock in their eyes gradually transformed into despair. They looked like they'd lost all will to live, their earlier cultivation enthusiasm completely gone.

It's not heartbreaking when someone better than you also works harder. What's truly heartbreaking is when someone who doesn't even try is ALWAYS better than you, the one who does!

"Old Xie, are we still gonna cultivate?"

"Cultivate my ass. Sleep! I'm gonna break through too!"

Xie Sunan flopped down to sleep, his heart already wounded by Chen Shu.

Xu Xingxing wore an equally melancholic expression, gazing at the moonlight outside the balcony, his heart full of sorrow.

He even considered asking his family to spend ten or twenty million to force a breakthrough...

Early the next morning.

"Ahhh, that felt great!"

Chen Shu felt refreshed and full of energy.

"What's up with you two?"

Chen Shu immediately noticed Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing sporting panda eyes, looking completely sleep-deprived.

Though the two had flopped down to sleep, they'd actually been awake all night.

Some things are like that—the more you think about them, the harder they are to let go...

Xie Sunan said expressionlessly, "Do you know what happened last night?"

Chen Shu looked around the dorm. "What? Did that giant python come back?!"

Xu Xingxing sighed and shook his head. "Did you... break through?"

Chen Shu checked his senses and immediately knew he'd successfully broken through to Level 8 Beast Tamer.

He said matter-of-factly, "Yeah, broke through. So what?"

"You're not surprised?"

The two stared wide-eyed, feeling their hearts take another critical hit.

Chen Shu pursed his lips. "What's the big deal? Level 8 Beast Tamer—anyone with hands can do it."

The two raised their heads to the heavens in lament. There was no living like this anymore. All their daily cultivation couldn't match this guy taking a nap.

Chen Shu said with a completely serious face, "I told you—sleeping and squatting on the toilet are both forms of cultivation. Believe me now?"

...

The three headed to the Beast Taming Arena.

Xie Sunan and Xu Xingxing looked utterly exhausted, their resentful gazes fixed on Chen Shu, drawing strange looks from everyone around them.

Shen Wushuang walked to the front, about to speak, when someone suddenly raised their hand.

"Hm? Du Long, what is it?"

"Teacher! Today I want to challenge Chen Shu!"

Du Long's eyes were full of confidence as he looked provocatively at Chen Shu below.

"Since you said only the strongest can be class monitor, I think that should be me!"

Du Long instantly summoned his Earth Sub-dragon, looking majestic and imposing.

"Chen Shu, what do you say?"

Shen Wushuang's gaze turned to Chen Shu, a faint smile at the corner of his lips.

The special training class should be full of competition. Only through rivalry could students reach their full potential.

*Just broke through and someone wants to walk into the gun barrel... no wait, cannon's fire?*

"I can accept, but there needs to be at least some barrier to entry. What, any random nobody can just challenge me now?"

Chen Shu spread his hands and sighed. "I need time to study too, Teacher!"

"Fair point." Shen Wushuang nodded. "Then let's use one Beast Orb as the wager. How about that?"

Special training class students received one High-Grade Beast Orb every month to accelerate their cultivation speed.

"No objections!"

Du Long nodded slightly, confidence gleaming in his eyes.

Though only a week had passed, his understanding of skills had undergone a qualitative leap.

Options suddenly appeared before Chen Shu's eyes.

[Option 1: Refuse the match while asking with a straight face: "Are you even worthy?" Reward: Medium Beast Taming Power]

[Option 2: Accept the match and beat the Earth Sub-dragon senseless! Reward: 5 Power Flowers]

[Option 3: Pretend to refuse, then launch a sneak attack! Strike first, show off that Bandit swagger! Reward: Rebound Force skill +1]

Translator's Notes:

[1] "A decision that dishonors my ancestors" (做出一个违背祖宗的决定): This is a 2020 Chinese internet meme originating from a viral video of a rural man being pressured into taking a high-interest loan. He dramatically declared he was making "a decision that betrays his ancestors" before signing. The phrase became widely used to describe any reluctant compromise, especially involving money.
